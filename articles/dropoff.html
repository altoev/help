Drop Off

At Altoev, we strive to provide our customers with an exceptional experience. As your reservation comes to a close, we would like to express our gratitude for choosing us for your vehicle rental needs. Below are important details regarding the conclusion of your reservation and vehicle return process.

Reservation Conclusion

We hope this message finds you well and that you have enjoyed your experience with our vehicle. As your reservation is set to end at the designated time, we would like to remind you of a few key points.

Extending Your Reservation

If you require additional time with the vehicle, you are welcome to extend your reservation. Should the current vehicle be unavailable for an extension, we encourage you to explore other options within our fleet, subject to availability.

Vehicle Return Instructions

If you choose not to extend your reservation, please ensure that you return the vehicle to the designated pickup location by the reserved ending time. For those who do not have the prepaid charging option, it is essential to return the vehicle with at least a 70% charge.

Feedback and Support

Thank you once again for choosing Altoev for your journey. We hope that our service has contributed positively to your travels. If you have any questions or require assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@altoev.com.

Share Your Experience

We would greatly appreciate it if you could take a moment to leave a positive review on Turo, should you have enjoyed your experience with us. Your feedback helps us continue to provide excellent service. Additionally, we invite you to participate in our survey at https://altoev.com/survey.

Thank you for your cooperation, and we look forward to serving you again in the future!
