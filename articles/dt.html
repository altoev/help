Discount Tire Information

At Altoev, we prioritize the safety and satisfaction of our customers. In the event of tire issues, we have established a partnership with Discount Tire to ensure you receive prompt and efficient service. Below are the steps to follow if you encounter any tire problems during your rental period.

Steps to Address Tire Problems

If you experience any tire issues, please follow these instructions:

  1. Tow or navigate your vehicle to the nearest Discount Tire location.
  2. Upon arrival, provide the staff with our Discount Tire Fleet Account Number: 65141.
  3. If the tire can be repaired, please have it fixed at no cost to you.
  4. If the tire requires replacement, ensure that it is replaced with the same or a similar tire.

More Information

For more information about Discount Tire services, please visit their website at Discount Tire.
