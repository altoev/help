Roadside Assistance

In the event that you require roadside assistance while using an Altoev rental vehicle, it is important to follow the outlined steps to ensure a smooth and efficient process. This guide will provide you with the necessary actions to take when you find yourself in need of help.

Steps to Follow for Roadside Assistance

  1. Ensure Your Safety
    First and foremost, make sure you are stopped in a safe location. If you are not in a safe place, please find one before proceeding.
  2. Record Your Vehicle Information
    On the Tesla screen, click the Car Icon, navigate to "Software", and take note of the Vehicle Identification Number (VIN). Specifically, focus on the last eight digits of the VIN, as this information will be required for assistance.
  3. Locate Your Roadside Assistance Policy Number
    The policy number is located in the envelope in the center console under the armrest. Please retrieve it before contacting roadside assistance.
  4. Contact Roadside Assistance
    Using your cell phone, call our dedicated roadside assistance number at (800) 503-9643.
  5. Wait for Assistance
    Once you have made the call, please wait for the tow truck to arrive. It is crucial that you do not leave the vehicle until help arrives.

Common Reasons for Roadside Assistance

  • Car ran out of battery — Tow to the nearest supercharger.
  • Car has a flat tire — Tow to the nearest Discount Tire. (More information)
  • When in doubt — Tow to the nearest Tesla Service Center.
  • Locked out — Please contact us.

Important Note

If you were in any type of accident, do not call for roadside assistance.

By following these steps, you can ensure that you receive the necessary assistance promptly and safely.
