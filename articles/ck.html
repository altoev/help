Charge Key FAQ

Charge Key is an innovative solution designed to enhance your rental experience with Altoev. This digital car key allows you to access your rental vehicle effortlessly, eliminating the need for physical keys or additional accounts. In this document, we will explore the features of Charge Key, how to use it, payment processes, and more.

What is Charge Key?

Charge Key is a digital car key that provides you with access to your rental vehicle through a simple link. This technology streamlines the rental process, allowing you to charge your vehicle at a Supercharger with payments automatically processed using your saved payment method. There is no need to pay at the charger or request reimbursements later.

How do I use Charge Key?

Using Charge Key is straightforward. Follow these steps to access your vehicle:

  • Altoev will send you a secure digital key link.
  • Click the link to access your vehicle throughout your rental period.
  • Download the Charge Key app for seamless Bluetooth unlocking.
  • Enjoy automatic charging payments—no manual transactions needed.

Do I need to download the app?

While it is possible to use Charge Key through your web browser, we highly recommend downloading the app for enhanced functionality. The app offers:

  • Hands-free unlocking and locking with Bluetooth.
  • A more reliable connection to your vehicle.
  • Functionality even in areas with poor cell service.

You can download the Charge Key app from:

  • iPhone: Get it on the Apple App Store.
  • Android: Get it on the Google Play Store.

How do I pay for charging?

When you use a Supercharger, the charging cost is automatically billed to your payment method on file. This means you do not need to manually pay at the charger, simplifying the process for you.

What payment methods can I use with Charge Key?

Charge Key accepts all major credit and debit cards, including:

  • Visa
  • Mastercard
  • American Express
  • Discover

A transaction says "Pending." What does that mean?

If a transaction appears as "Pending," it may indicate that the payment processing encountered an issue. If you have any concerns or require assistance, please reach out to us at ckhelp@altoev.com or text us at (833) 425-8638.
