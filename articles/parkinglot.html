Getting to Our Parking Lot

We’re excited to help you get started on your journey. If you're arriving at the Jacksonville International Airport and need to find our parking lot, follow these simple directions to make your way there with ease.

Directions

To reach the Altoev parking lot from the airport, please follow these steps:

  1. As you face the rental car counters (such as Hertz, Enterprise, etc.), head all the way to the left until you reach the far left door. This door leads to the pre-arranged parking lot.
  2. Walk up the ramp towards the car entrance and exit until you arrive at the "Security Booth" and the entrance/exit gate.
  3. Continue walking past the gate and straight across the street towards the "Sheltair" sign.
  4. Make your way up towards the "Sheltair" building.
  5. Finally, head towards the back right side of the parking lot, as indicated in the image below.

Address

If you need more assistance, please enter the address below into your desired "Maps" app and select the walking navigation. Be sure to select the walking navigation, not the driving. The driving will send you the long way.

14600 Whirlwind Avenue
Jacksonville, FL 32218

Need Assistance?

We hope these directions help you find us easily. If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out. Safe travels!
