How to Book a Reservation

Edited 7 days ago

At Altoev, we strive to make the reservation process as seamless as possible. Whether you prefer to book online, via email, or over the phone, we have multiple options available to suit your needs. Below are the detailed methods for securing your reservation.

Email Reservation

You can book a reservation by emailing us at book@altoev.com. Please include the following information in your email:

  • Starting Date & Time
  • Ending Date & Time
  • Desired Budget & Vehicle (if applicable)

Phone Reservation

If you prefer to speak with someone directly, you can call us at 833-425-8638 and select option 1. Please have the following information ready:

  • Starting Date & Time
  • Ending Date & Time
  • Desired Budget & Vehicle (if applicable)
  • Credit or Debit Card Information

Online Booking

You can also book a vehicle directly through our website. Please visit http://altoev.com/book and follow the steps provided to complete your reservation.

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any issues while booking a reservation online, please do not hesitate to reach out to us via email or phone. We are here to help you every step of the way.
