Pickup

Welcome to Altoev! We’re excited to help you get on the road with your rental vehicle. Below, you’ll find all the essential information you need for a smooth pickup and access experience. Let’s get started!

Pickup Location

Your vehicle will be available for pickup at Jacksonville International Airport. We look forward to seeing you there!

For more assistance in finding the pickup location, please use our parking lot help page here.

Cleanliness

Your rental vehicle has been thoroughly cleaned and sanitized for your safety. We take cleanliness seriously to ensure you have a pleasant experience.

Vehicle Charge

The vehicle will be fully charged upon your arrival, so you can hit the road without any delays!

Driver Requirements

The primary driver must present a valid driver’s license at the time of pickup. This is to ensure a smooth and secure rental process.

Verification

To verify eligibility, please provide photos of all drivers’ licenses along with selfies holding them. This helps us confirm that everything is in order for your rental.

Charge Key

The vehicle does not have a physical key. Instead, we recommend downloading the Charge Key app for a seamless key-free experience. Here’s how it works:

  • Charge Key will request a card for supercharging costs, authorizing $1.00 initially and billing the card after charging.
  • Please note that Charge Key is currently in Beta and may have some issues. If you encounter any problems, feel free to contact us!

For more information, visit our Charge Key help page here.

Download the Charge Key app from the following links:

  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play Store

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions or concerns, don’t hesitate to reach out! We’re here to help you every step of the way.
